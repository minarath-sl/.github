**Minarath** is a digital platform designed for Sri Lanka’s Muslim community. It serves as a unified system that connects individuals and mosques through three main components:

* **For Users:** A consumer application providing access to local prayer times, Islamic tools, mosque discovery, and community content.
* **For Mosques:** A dedicated management dashboard for mosque operators to handle jamaat details, broadcast announcements, manage facility information, and coordinate contributions.
* **For Administrators:** A governance layer designed to oversee mosque registrations and monitor overall platform health.
